Advice to 1st, 2nd, 3rd Year students
Not much advice for 1st years, just have fun, make friends and cherish every moment because you will never get that kind of time and opportunity again. These are the memories that stay with you forever so make the best of it. “A little nerd alert here” 2nd year is when we all become seniors and I agree that is a nice feeling but it’s also the year where we have the most boring and difficult subjects to pass so I would advise to get a little serious about attending classes. Apart from that I suggest you guys attend other college fests too not just because we don’t have one but because it’s really fun and gives a lot of exposure.
I don’t think 3rd years would really need any advice because they have everything figured out or at least they think they do. So I would only ask them to never give up and pursue something they are passionate about. I know that balancing between FS classes and Jntu can be very challenging and nerve wracking but you just have to get used to it. This is probably the worst advice but you’ll know for yourself when you perform confidently during placements and that is when all the hard work pays off.
