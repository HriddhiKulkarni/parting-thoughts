5 things you wish KMIT had
A larger campus, a convocation ceremony, free Wi-Fi everywhere, the radio to be revived – trust me, I tried. pizza in the canteen. xD
Favourite Adda on the campus
The last benches in class – it’s the best spot, ever!
Advice to 1st, 2nd, 3rd Year students
Serious advice: Be nice to the profs. They’re really cool once you get to know them – and extremely helpful! Not-so-serious-advice: Ask that girl out. She’ll probably say yes!
Things everyone should do in KMIT
Go up to the Radio room at least once! There is some real good equipment there. Also, start doing something about it, kids.
Any fascinating person/people you’ve met at KMIT
Jayanth sir! He taught us one class on Python.. And after a few months, he met me near the R&D department. This was back in 1st year. He asked me why I chose engineering, to which I replied saying I had no choice, and it was the next best thing for me after my dreams of studying law didn’t materialize. He asked me to either develop an interest, or go do something else that I was passionate about. He was pretty loud when he exclaimed “WHAT?! Why would you do that to yourself? People like you should not pursure engineering!” I swear, the whole corridor looked at me weirdly. After that day, I was pretty sure I’d never forget him, or his advice.
What will you miss about college life or being at KMIT?
Definitely, all the fun stuff we used to do during class, in the back benches! Pretty sure we gave the profs a bit of a hard time! Also, I’ll miss my stint as a former Editor of kMitra, formerly Vipasana.
Best experience at KMIT:
I’m not one for bunking classes, but some of the best things I remember, are during the times we spent loitering near the library stairs, hiding from profs. In the blistering heat, we lazed around chatting about nothing and everything. Then, we panicked and started writing an assignment after someone came up to us and told us it was the last day of sumbmission. XD
Where do you see KMIT 5 years from now?
Expanding rapidly and extremely well known for its CSE courses. It’s already quite famous, and I’m sure it’ll easily become one of the best colleges in the state.
5 things that only happen in KMIT
Getting caught while bunking classes, Patang Utsav – one of my favourite things about the college, having started a college magazine from scratch – and running it so perfectly over a short period of time. The lovely festivities like the Halloween fests and KMIT Evening.
Favorite year in college
3rd year – 1st semester! It was one of the best ever. We had a lot of fun, the lecturers very lenient, and we had quite a lot of free time.
When have you been punished and for what?
When have you been punished and for what? I haven’t been punished. XD Haha! Call me a goody-two-shoes!
